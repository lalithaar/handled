    FAQ

    What counts as "one request"? +

    Any single problem you hand us — a churn diagnosis, an architecture review, a growth stall. We work it end-to-end before starting the next one.

    How fast will I get something back? +

    2–3 business days per request, depending on scope. You'll always know where it stands.

    How does onboarding work? +

    Subscribe and a Trello board is shared with you within a business day. No kickoff calls, no syncs — just start dropping requests.

    Who's actually doing the work? +

    A one-human agency, run by Lalitha A R. No other researchers, no outsourcing to any other entity — you work directly with me for the entirety of your subscription. Background in product, tech, and research.

    Is there a limit to how many requests I can make? +

    No limit. Queue up as many as you'd like — we work through them one at a time and move to the next the moment one wraps.

    How does the pause feature work? +

    Pause anytime and billing pauses with it. Come back whenever — no rejoining fee, no lost history.

    How do you handle larger requests? +

    We break it down into smaller requests on the Trello board on your behalf, so nothing sits as one giant unmoving card.

    What tools do you work in? +

    Whatever fits the request — diagrams and docs for architecture calls, code specs for engineering handoffs, spreadsheets for growth/ops analysis.

    Can you help implement the recommendation too? +

    No — implementation stays with your team. You know your product best, and your A-players are the right ones to build it. My job ends at the ranked call.

    How will I send requests? +

    Through a Trello board — you'll get a template to drop requests into, one card per request.

    What if I don't like the recommendation? +

    Revisions are unlimited and included. We iterate until it's something your team will actually ship.

    Are there any requests you don't support? +

    If it needs research, options, and a call, we can take it on. Pure execution work with no decision attached, testing implementations, and running user interviews are outside what's handled here.

    What if I only have a single request? +

    Subscribe for the month, send that one request, then pause. You're never locked into a longer commitment than you need.

    Are there any refunds? +

    No refunds on the current billing cycle, but you can pause or cancel anytime for the next one — no questions asked.

    Can I use Handled for just a month? +

    Yes. It's month to month, no contract — stay for one cycle or ten.
